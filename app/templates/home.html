How it works

Encoding

When you put in some input text, an algorithm converts that input text to 1s and 0s.
The 'Binary-0' field in the encoder represents what a 0 in binary will be in your custom encoding.
The 'Binary-1' field in the encoder represents what a 1 in binary will be in your custom encoding.

NOTE: DO NOT PUT SPACES IN THE VALUES FOR BINARY-0 OR BINARY-1!!!

Decoding

Decoding works the same way, but in reverse. Make sure to input the correct binary-0 and binary-1 values used to encode the text.
